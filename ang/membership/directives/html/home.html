Compucorp Front End Developer Tasks

Please read these instructions carefully as there are 2 “routes” depending on your preferred specialism.

Route 1: Javascript route

In route 1 we will test principly your JS and ability to adapt to CiviCRM development environment. HTML and CSS will be assessed as part of the technical interview in more detail.

If you wish to go down route 1 please ensure that tasks 1 and 2 are completed.

You may optionally wish to complete task 3 also, but making a working Drupal site and theme are optional.

Route 2: Styling route

In route 2 we will test primarily your CSS and HTML skills, especially ability to produce pixel perfect responsive designs, but also your ability to adapt to the Drupal and CiviCRM development environments. JS will be assessed as part of the technical interview in more detail.

If you wish to take route 2 please complete Task 3 and task 2 but creating a working Drupal website with theme for Task 3 is mandatory.

If you have any questions on these routes or tasks please let us know.
